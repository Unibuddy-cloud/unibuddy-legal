Account Deletion

Permanently remove your account and associated data directly from the app — no forms, no waiting.

How to Delete Your Account
Follow these steps inside the app
1
Open the UniBuddy app Launch the app on your device and sign in if prompted.
2
Go to More ··· Find the "More" tab in the bottom navigation bar.
3
Tap "Delete Account" Scroll to the account settings section and tap the delete option.
4
Confirm deletion Read the confirmation prompt and confirm. This action cannot be undone.
What Gets Deleted
Data removed when your account is deleted
Firebase Authentication Account Your login credentials and authentication record are permanently removed.
User Profile Data Your name, bio, university details, and profile photo are deleted.
User-Generated Content Posts, comments, and other content associated with your account may also be removed.
Cached Local App Data Temporary Some app data cached on your device may remain until you uninstall the app.
This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please ensure you've saved anything you may need before proceeding.
