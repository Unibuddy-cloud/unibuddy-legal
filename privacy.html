Introduction

Welcome to Unibuddy — your campus companion application designed to help students connect, trade, discover events, and navigate college life with ease.

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our application. Please read it carefully.

ℹ

By accessing or using Unibuddy, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with its terms, please discontinue use of the application.

2

Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register and use Unibuddy, we may collect the following personal details:

  • Full name
  • Email address (college/university email preferred)
  • Course or program of study
  • College or university affiliation

2.2 User-Generated Content

Content you create or submit within the app, including:

  • Items listed in Campus Shop (buy/sell posts, descriptions, photos)
  • Lost & Found listings and related details
  • Event-related participation data or submissions

2.3 Usage Data

We also automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Login activity and session timestamps
  • App interactions and feature usage patterns
  • Basic, non-sensitive device information (e.g., OS type, app version)
3

How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect solely to improve your experience and operate the application effectively. Specifically:

  • Provide and maintain core application functionality
  • Enable Campus Shop to connect buyers and sellers within your campus
  • Manage and display Lost & Found listings
  • Display, manage, and promote campus events
  • Improve user experience and optimize app performance
  • Send important notifications including updates, announcements, and alerts
4

Campus Shop Disclaimer

The Campus Shop feature is designed to connect students within the same campus community. Please be aware of the following:

  • Unibuddy acts only as a platform connecting buyers and sellers — we are not a retailer or marketplace operator
  • We do not own, sell, verify, or endorse any listed items
  • We are not responsible for transactions, payments, disputes, or any loss arising between users
  • Users are strongly advised to transact safely and exercise their own judgment
⚠

Always meet in safe, public locations on campus when completing transactions. Unibuddy is not liable for any disputes arising from Campus Shop interactions.

5

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for commercial purposes.

We may share data only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — such as Google, for authentication (sign-in) and secure database storage
  • Legal authorities — only if required by applicable law, court order, or regulatory requirement
✓

Your personal data is never sold to advertisers or third-party marketing platforms.

6

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. These include:

  • Secure authentication mechanisms to protect your account
  • Encrypted data transmission over HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Controlled access to databases and stored information
⚠

Please note that no system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential.

User Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile.

Deletion

Delete your account and all associated personal data.

Content Removal

Request removal of specific content you have posted.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 12.

Content Responsibility

Users are solely responsible for the accuracy, legality, and appropriateness of content they post on Unibuddy. We reserve the right to review and remove content that:

  • Violates our Terms of Service or community policies
  • Is misleading, fraudulent, or deceptive
  • Is harmful, offensive, abusive, or otherwise inappropriate

Events Information

Event data displayed within the Unibuddy app may be provided by colleges, student groups, clubs, or individual users. While we strive to keep event information current and accurate:

  • We do not guarantee the accuracy or completeness of event listings
  • We are not responsible for cancellations, changes, or inaccuracies in third-party event data
  • We encourage users to verify event details through official college channels
10

Children's Privacy

Unibuddy is intended exclusively for college and university students. The application is not directed at children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us right away.

Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we do:

  • The "Effective Date" at the top of this page will be updated accordingly
  • For significant changes, we may notify you via in-app notification or email

Continued use of Unibuddy after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
